# Coming Soon: Roasted Heirloom Tomato Sauce

Every August, New England farm stands overflow with heirloom tomatoes in every shape and color — Brandywines, Green Zebras, Cherokee Purples. We slow-roast them until they're deeply caramelized and concentrated, then finish with fresh basil and good olive oil. The result is the best pizza sauce we've ever made, and it only exists for about six weeks a year. Recipe coming soon.
